MUSIC

Now or Never Now : Metric

A very close friend of mine introduced the "Art of Doubt" album to me, and I give it significant importance because of that. Metric has a very nice sound; this song reminds me of peace.

Vacation Town : The Front Bottoms

Having listened to this band for over 6 years, I can confidently say I will never get tired of this song! There is an incredibly nostalgic tone with the chorus and I enjoy listening to it when I'd like to think of good experiences.

Love Words IV : Deco*27

This song is very important to me! It shares a similar tone of nostalgia; the lyrics express comfort and was released by the author as an expression of appreciation for his listeners throughout the span of his career.

GAMES

Detroit : Become Human

When I finished this game in 2019, I discovered key aspects of what truly makes a good game. I was captivated by the story behind each of its characters and fully immersed from beginning to end, as each decision made by the player is significant and impactful.

OFF

This game, despite having numerous flaws, tackles the premise in a way that is able to fully deliver an entertaining experience. The player's experience of the game is altered by each conflict as the story progresses to ultimately reveal the rose-tinted glasses that had been driving the player's view since the start. I enjoy games with complex stories and empathetic characters.

MOVIES

Hereditary

This movie is one of, if not my absolute favourite horror movie of all time. It doesn't rely on cheap jumpscares or visual effects- instead captivating the viewer with its realistic portrayal of a broken family and instilling an innate sense of dread through the dynamics the film does a great job of exploring using paranormal concepts.

The Menu

This movie is not as much scary, as it is intriguing and very comedic. The film unfolds a commentary on the food service industry, and it has a little bit of everything for all viewers.
